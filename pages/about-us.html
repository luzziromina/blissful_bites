About us

Blissful Bites is not just a donut store; it's an embodiment of passion, creativity, and community in Portland, Oregon. Founded in 2003 by lifelong friends Kenneth "Cat Daddy" Pogsonand Tres Shannon, they aimed to create unconventional and joy-inducing donuts for their customers.

From its humble beginnings, Blissful Bites quickly became an iconic local treasure and a must-visit destination for donut enthusiasts from near and far. With a focus on pushing the boundaries of flavor and design, our team of passionate bakers and artists continuously dreams up innovative and playful donut creations that leave a lasting impression.

Blissful Bites goes beyond its donuts; it serves as a beacon of community and philanthropy. By partnering with local charities and organizations, we aim to make a positive impact, promoting awareness for social issues, and spreading kindness and joy within our extended family of customers.
