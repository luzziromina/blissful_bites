About us

In the vibrant city of Portland, Oregon, we are proud to run a beloved donut store known as Voodoo Doughnut. Our journey began in 2003 when two friends, Kenneth "Cat Daddy" Pogson and Tres Shannon, joined forces to bring their unique donut creations to life.

Since then, our donut store has become a local sensation, captivating the hearts and taste buds of both residents and visitors. When you step into Voodoo Doughnut, you'll be greeted by our playful atmosphere and the irresistible aroma of our freshly baked treats. We take great delight in offering a wide range of flavors and toppings, from traditional favorites to daring and imaginative combinations.

From the moment you take your first bite, you'll understand why our donuts are truly special. We strive to create an experience that goes beyond simply satisfying your cravings; we want to make your visit to Voodoo Doughnut a moment of pure joy and indulgence.

As we continue to grow, we remain committed to our roots and our community. We cherish the support we receive and always look for ways to give back. Whether it's collaborating with local organizations or supporting important causes, we are dedicated to making a positive impact.

We invite you to join us on this delightful journey. Come and experience the magic of Voodoo Doughnut firsthand. Indulge in our playful designs, discover new flavor combinations, and allow yourself to be transported to a world of pure donut bliss. We can't wait to welcome you with open arms and treat you to an unforgettable donut experience
